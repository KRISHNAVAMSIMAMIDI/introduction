FILM MAKING

Filmmakers can use their platforms to bring important conversations to their audiences by creating stories that highlight various social or political events, injustices or inspiring stories that can inform as much as they entertain. Filmmaking occurs in a variety of economic, social, and political context around the world, and uses a variety of technologies and cinematic techniques to make theatrical films, episodic films for television and streaming platforms, music videos, and promotional and educations films.
